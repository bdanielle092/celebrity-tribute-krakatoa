Celebrity Tribute to Bela Fleck

Concert News

"For Fleck's show at Strathmore he'll be joined by the best of the new generation of bluegrass players, including the acclaimed guitarist Bryan Sutton, mandolin virtuoso Sierra Hull, 10-time International Bluegrass Music Association Fiddle Player of the Year Michael Cleveland, along with bassist/multi-instrumentalist Mark Schatz."

Recent Events

"Banjo House Lockdown is a Facebook Live series from Béla Fleck & Abigail Washburn broadcast from their home in Nashville, TN on Friday nights. The first episode aired March 20th with scheduled episodes followed on 3/27, 4/3, and 4/10."

Bluegrass Nation

"As a teen, Bela Fleck first became attracted to the Bluegrass through Earl Scruggs and his "Beverly Hillbillies" theme song. When a Chick Corea concert piqued his interest in fusion, Fleck began innovating the banjo's role in bluegrass, moving to Nashville in the early '80s to play with the New Grass Revival."
